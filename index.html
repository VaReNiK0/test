What We Repair

Expert Repair for
Every Appliance

Our certified technicians service all major brands — Whirlpool, GE, Samsung, LG, Bosch, Maytag, and more.

🧊

Refrigerator Repair

Not cooling? Leaking water? Strange noises? We diagnose and fix compressor, ice maker, thermostat, and seal issues — fast.

🫧

Washing Machine Repair

Won't spin, drain, or start? We repair top-load and front-load washers including drum, pump, belt, and control board issues.

🔥

Oven & Stove Repair

Burner won't ignite? Oven not heating evenly? We fix gas and electric ranges, baking elements, igniters, and control boards.

🍽️

Dishwasher Repair

Dishes still dirty or water not draining? We repair spray arms, pumps, door latches, and water inlet valves for all brands.

💨

Dryer Repair

Dryer not heating or taking too long? We service heating elements, thermostats, drum belts, and blower fans for gas & electric units.

🌬️

Microwave Repair

No power, sparking, or not heating? We safely diagnose magnetrons, capacitors, door switches, and control panels.

🧺

Freezer Repair

Frost buildup or temperature inconsistencies? We repair defrost systems, fan motors, and seals for chest and upright freezers.

🍦

Ice Maker Repair

Ice maker stopped working or producing too much ice? We fix water supply lines, ice molds, and module boards efficiently.
